^YFlappy^0 for MZ-800
(c) dB-Soft 1984

Logic game - move blue
ball to blue wall.

^WRIGHT^0 ... right
^WUP^0 ...... up
^WLEFT^0 .... left
^WDOWN^0 .... down
^WA^0 ....... shoot mushroom
^WX^0 ....... solve scene
    (only first 8 scenes)
^WY^0 ....... quit

Support USB keyboard:
A->Ctrl, B->Alt, X->Shift,
Y->Esc
