^YMandelbrot^0

Fractal pattern generator.
Uses both CPU cores.

^WUP^0 .......... move up
^WLEFT^0 ........ move left
^WRIGHT^0 ....... move right
^WDOWN^0 ........ move down
^WA+UP^0 ........ zoom in
^WA+DOWN^0 ...... zoom out
^WA+RIGHT^0 ..... incr. steps
^WA+LEFT^0 ...... decr. steps
^WY^0 ........... quit

Blinking LED indicates
render completed.

Support USB keyboard:
A->Ctrl, B->Alt, X->Shift,
Y->Esc
