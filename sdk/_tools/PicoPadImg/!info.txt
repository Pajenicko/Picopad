Input image must be in Windows-BMP 24-bit, 8-bit or 4-bit color format, no compress, switch ON flip row order.

To generate uncompressed image 16-bit, 8-bit or 4-bit:
  PicoPadImg input.bmp output.c name

To generate RLE compressed image 8-bit or 4-bit:
  PicoPadImg input.bmp output.c name r
